```python
#forcing for fixed emission estimates
emvals = np.array([0.,5.,12.5,25.,50.,100.])*4. # for 4 regions
```

```python
ne = np.linspace(emvals.min(), emvals.max(), num=20, endpoint=True)
```

```python
f1 = np.interp(em1, emvals, forcvals)
```

